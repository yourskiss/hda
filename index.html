About HDA

The Hedera DeFi Alliance has been created with a clear mission in mind. Its purpose is to allow industry-leading projects and their respective team to connect, collaborate, and collectively contribute to the emergence of a healthy and sustainable global DeFi ecosystem built on Hedera. DeFi lowers the barrier for a greater audience to access financial products but equally serves to unlock liquidity for products and services that would otherwise not be possible.

By providing a trusted platform within the Hedera network, the HDA is driving the movement for global adoption of DeFi technology and cementing the foundation for a transparent, cost-effective, low-energy decentralized financial system.
We offer guidance and support to existing as well as new DeFi platforms building on Hedera and share our experience and knowledge to nurture thought leadership and engage in impactful initiatives that produce meaningful solutions for real-world cases.
